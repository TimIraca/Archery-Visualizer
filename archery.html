70-Metre Olympic Archery Visualizer

Shoot a full 72-arrow match or run twenty at once. The simulator starts from measured 70 m hit coordinates, then scales that real impact pattern toward Olympic-style 72-arrow score levels: pro, elite, intermediate, and beginner.
